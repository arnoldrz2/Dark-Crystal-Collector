Dark Crystal Collector

At the start of the game, you will be given a random number as your Target Number.


There will be character boxes below. By clicking on the boxes you will add a specific amount of points to Your Number.


You win the game by matching your Your Number to Target Number, you lose the game if your Your Number goes above the Target Number.


The value of each character box is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each character box.
